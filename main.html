Summary

Inception is a 2010 science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows a skilled thief, Dom Cobb, who enters people's dreams to steal secrets. He is offered a chance to have his past crimes forgiven if he can successfully implant an idea into someone's mind, a process known as inception.
